Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How do I get a quote?
Fill out our quote request form below, call or text us at (937) 562-1235, or email walnutandwilsonhomes@gmail.com. We respond within 1 business day with a free estimate.
What areas do you serve?
We provide full service in the Dayton and Cincinnati metro areas, including surrounding communities. Contact us if you're outside these areas — we may still be able to help.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. We are fully licensed and insured, with specific trade licenses for plumbing, electrical, and gas work. Our lead manager has 20+ years of residential and commercial experience.
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope. Small repairs can often be done in a day, while full remodels (kitchens, bathrooms, basements) typically take 2–6 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before work begins.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes. For projects that require permits, we handle the entire process including pulling permits and scheduling inspections. We also specialize in pre- and post-inspection repairs.
Can I see examples of your work?
Absolutely! Check out our project gallery to see recent work including basement transformations, kitchen remodels, bathroom rebuilds, and more.
